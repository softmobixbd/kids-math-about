Privacy Policy for QuickMath Quest

Effective Date:1 Julay 2025

Welcome to QuickMath Quest, developed by SoftMobiXBD. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information in our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from users.

However, the app may collect the following non-personal information:

  • Anonymous gameplay data (e.g., scores, level progress)
  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version) for performance analysis

2. Use of Data

The information collected is used only to:

  • Improve game performance
  • Fix bugs and enhance user experience
  • Track gameplay statistics anonymously

We do not use this data for advertising or tracking individual users.

3. Third-Party Services

QuickMath Quest may use trusted third-party services that may collect non-personal data:

  • Google Play Services – For app functionality and analytics
  • Hotpot.ai – Used for graphic generation (only by developers)
  • Pixabay.com – For royalty-free sound assets (does not involve user data)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal data without your consent, please contact us so we can remove the information.

5. Data Storage & Security

We do not store any user data on our servers. All user progress is stored locally on the device. If the app is uninstalled, this data may be lost.

6. Permissions

QuickMath Quest does not require access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location. The app runs independently without accessing sensitive device features.

7. Ads and Monetization

We may display non-personalized ads using AdMob to support free access to the game. These ads are designed to be age-appropriate. No personal data is collected for ad personalization.

8. Data Deletion Requests

Since we do not collect or store user data, there is nothing to delete. However, if you have questions or concerns, you may contact us anytime.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
